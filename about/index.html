About

Everyone loves an origin story.

History

Giant Machines originated in 2012 as a collaboration between David Michael, Frank Cantelmi, and Roy Yang where they were a core team of engineers overhauling a real-time messaging system for a Fortune 1000 company. Building on the success of that project, they realized they cast a large technical shadow, from UI/UX to product expertise and engineering. Since then, Giant Machines has grown into a multi-disciplined team of designers and engineers that specialize in highly customized websites and mobile applications.

Our Expertise

Enterprise

We specialize in enterprise grade software, whether it be connecting with your existing ERP or migrating your your production database to the cloud along with building API capabilities.

Financial Tech

With over 20 years of experience in fintech, our principals understand finance nuances from compliance to redundancy. We have a range of experiences, from building bond price parsing models to real time financial messaging applications.

Ecommerce

We have helped our clients streamline their ecommerce offerings, adding a level of sophistication to the online shopping experience while improving inventory and business intelligence.

iOS and WatchOS

Our team has deployed several iOS and WatchOS apps, some with over one million downloads. We can help you build your mobile app and the web services to support it.
