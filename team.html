History

Giant Machines originated in 2012 as a collaboration between David Michael, Frank Cantelmi, and Roy Yang where they were a core team of engineers overhauling a real-time messaging system for a Fortune 1000 company. Building on the success of that project, they realized they cast a large technical shadow, from UI/UX to product expertise and engineering. Since then, Giant Machines has grown into a multi-disciplined team of designers and engineers that specialize in highly customized websites and mobile applications.

Hiring

Front End Developer

Giant Machines is seeking a mid to senior front end developer to join our growing business.

Ideally we are interested in speaking with people who have at least 3 - 5 years of professional experience who can build a beautiful website that also looks good under the hood. Facility with Ruby is a must at the moment, but extra points for iOS.

As an front end developer at Giant Machines, you will work directly with clients using technologies appropriate for each specific challenge. Over the past year we have written code in Ruby, Go, C#/.NET, C++, Objective C/iOS, JavaScript and CoffeeScript, and designed products using Photoshop, Sketch, InVision, Balsamiq and plain old pen and paper. You need to have an interest in the above mentioned technologies but you must be versed in:

  • HTML5/CSS3 including Sass, SCSS and tools like Compass

  • Mobile web and responsive/adaptive design

  • Javascript libraries including jQuery, Backbone, Angular

Technology

We most commonly work with Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Coffeescript, Backbone and Sass. We build APIs to power our front-end web clients, iOS apps, and third-party integrations. We fix legacy systems by isolating them behind modern web technologies, and expose their data using modern, up-to-date practices.

We’re primarily a tech driven company, but use Photoshop, Sketch, Pixelmator, Balsamiq and old fashioned pen and paper to plan and create experiences.

We’re not process driven, but results driven.

About Us

Giant Machines is a technology consultancy in NYC. Quality is our guiding principle, and we work fast. Our team has experience in design, project management, front-end web development, back-end web development, and application development.

We’re looking for team members that are quick thinking, talented, flexible, and looking to grow their skill set.

Our company culture values drive, experimentation, curiosity. We strive to maintain work life balance, and care deeply about preventing burn out.
