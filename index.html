Development process

Our approach to software development selects aspects from many different development methodologies including Agile and Kanban to deliver value. For fresh projects we prefer short iterations, continuous integration and delivery, and common-sense pairing with daily standups and retrospectives to taste.

We understand that your organization is unique and can peacefully coexist with your existing processes. If your process is broken, we can help with that too.


Technical expertise
